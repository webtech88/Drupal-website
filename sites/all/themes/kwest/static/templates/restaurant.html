Kanteen restaurant

Featuring luxurious leather seating in rich blues and reds, against dark wood, stainless steel and glass, Kanteen is a stylish restaurant for contemporary dining. Chef Eduart Berisha heads up the team and offers a tempting menu full of creative modern dishes. Art in Kanteen We have joined forces with Laura McNamara Ltd to re-invigorate Kanteen with a fresh supply of original artwork, available for sale, every 6 months.
